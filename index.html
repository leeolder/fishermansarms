Maidstones First Craft Beer Bar

Welcome to The Fishermans Arms, the friendliest addition to Maidstone's vibrant pub scene! As the owner and Chief Brewer, Val is thrilled to open our doors to locals and visitors alike, inviting you to experience the charm and history of this beautiful location on the banks of the River Len.

We look forward to raising a glass with you and creating unforgettable memories in Maidstone Town Center's oldest and best rated pub!

Please feel free to contact us either by emailing info@fishermanspub.co.uk or by giving us a call at 01622 755443.

Our Beer

Indulge in our selection of locally crafted ales and ciders, each sip a celebration of the region's finest brews. Experience the rich flavors and distinctive character that highlight the craftsmanship of our area's breweries and cider makers.

Our bar also boasts a collection of wines, gins, and an array of spirits, ensuring a well-stocked selection for every discerning taste.

Homemade Food

Indulge in our delectable homemade cuisine available exclusively from Thursday through Sunday, where we take pride in crafting every dish with care and attention. Our extensive menu not only caters to diverse tastes but also showcases a substantial selection for our vegan patrons.

Where To Find Us

Conveniently tucked away on Lower Stone Street, you'll find The Fishermans Arms, a brief 3-minute stroll from the vibrant hub of Maidstone Town Center. Our location offers a tranquil escape within easy reach of the town's bustling energy.

Nestled under the beams of Maidstone's oldest pub, dating back to the 1430s, our establishment celebrates tradition while offering a modern and inviting atmosphere. Whether you're a craft beer enthusiast or simply looking to unwind with friends, The Fishermans Arms promises a memorable experience.
